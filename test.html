Fruits

Apple

Granny Smith, Royal Gala, Golden Delicious and Pink Lady are just a few of the thousands of different kinds of apple that are grown around the world!

Banana

Bananas make a nutritious snack! They are a great source of energy and contain lots of vitamins and minerals, especially potassium.

Apricot

Apricots can be eaten fresh or dried - both are packed with vitamins! Fresh apricots have a soft and slightly furry skin.
